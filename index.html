INFER Seminar Series

The INFER (Information, Networks, Feedback, Estimation, and Randomness) Seminar Series at the Dept. of EE, IIT Madras, hosts talks by leading researchers, academicians, and industry professionals around the world. The talks are structured to be pedagogical, at the pace of a classroom lecture, with a view towards accessibility by a diverse audience. Each talk will develop the central idea in sufficient technical detail so that the participants can directly proceed to the literature on the state-of-the-art. The seminars, each roughly 90 minutes in duration, will be primarily held online, and will be hosted on this YouTube playlist, by the NPTEL.
